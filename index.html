About Us

We are a team of passionate developers from UCP Institute of Technology pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science. We identified a critical gap in the tech community: many developers have strong coding skills but lack innovative ideas, while creators have brilliant concepts but limited technical abilities to bring them to life.

CodersMeet bridges this gap by connecting coders with creators, fostering collaboration that transforms ideas into reality. Our platform enables developers to find exciting projects while helping creators find the technical talent they need.
