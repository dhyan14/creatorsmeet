About Creators Meet

Creators Meet was born from a simple observation: many brilliant ideas never see the light of day due to lack of technical skills, while many talented developers are looking for meaningful projects to work on.

Our mission is to bridge this gap and create a community where ideas and skills can come together to create amazing things.
